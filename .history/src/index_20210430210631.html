Let’s be real, we're all a little gross. We share chapsticks and sniff socks when no one is looking. Luckily, we can get away with these unhygienic habits with Airmune, formulated to help support your immune system1, because we're only human and we're gross sometimes.

VITAMINS AND MINERALS

Each Airmune Effervescent Tablet delivers a blast of 1000 mg vitamin C as well as high quantities of vitamin A and E.

Vitamin C acts as an antioxidant supporting immune function. Vitamins A and E are factors of good health and contribute to the function of the immune system2. Airmune is also a source of Zinc and Selenium as well as magnesium, and manganese.

HERBAL BLEND

Herbal blend containing amongst others Ginger and Echinacea

Echinacea is used as a traditional herbal remedy for upper respirator tract infections. Ginger, Ionicera, forsythia, isatis root and Echinacea may have an effect on cold symptoms as a result of an anti-inflammatory effect3
